Laid on sand mixed with lime or cement, broken or sawn paving stones have been used for thousands of years for roads and squares for vehicles. The sections of 4/6 cm, 6/8 cm, 10/10 cm and the different stone colors give the possibility of creating graphic and unique pavings.
