# Welcome to Maroc Mosaic

**MAROC MOSAIC (DGMD sarl)** is a company specializing in the decoration of outdoor and indoor spaces. For 40 years, it has been installing its stone or marble mosaic floors, walls, and domes, as well as traditional fountains, on prestigious construction sites across Europe, Morocco, and the Middle East. 

The company uses the finest natural materials to create exceptional decorations and Andalusian and Roman atmospheres, inspired by the history of the Alhambra and Pompeii.
